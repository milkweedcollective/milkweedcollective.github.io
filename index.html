about us

The Milkweed Collective is a co-living, vegan, shared-housing and resources project, currently residing in a house called the Meadow in so-called East Vancouver. We formed in mid 2017, and have been living together in different ways ever since!

hello there

The Milkweed collecitve is seeking new housemates for 2022! Multiple options are possible - $675 for The Burrow( ), $500 for the Cat's Cradle(), or $1125 total to rent both! Every person also contributes $140-200 into the house fund, which pays for all utilites, bulk household supplies, annual farm produce membership, home improvement projects, and mutual aid contributions.

We have up to 2 rooms available for the right fit. We're excited about artists, introverts, extroverts, gardeners, vegans, and home tenders. Folks who are self-motivated, grounded in who they are, can step into the magic of co-creating a household, and enjoy both time alone and time with housemates tend to enjoy living here the most!
